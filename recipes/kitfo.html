kitfo


kitfo is served with injera, a spongy, absorbent
sourdough crepe-style bread made from fermented teff
flour, although in traditional Gurage cuisine, one
would use kocho, a thick flatbread made from the ensete
plant. An ensete leaf may be used as a garnish.
